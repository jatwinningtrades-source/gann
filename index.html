Mastering GANN Zones

GANN Zones Pro is a mathematical volatility strategy based on the Square of 144. By dividing the Prior Day's range into an 8-step "Octave," we identify precise institutional reversal and acceleration points.

Core Concept: Price doesn't move randomly. It moves between mathematical vibrations defined by previous volatility.

Level Hierarchy

Not all levels are equal. Your trade quality depends on which level is being tested.

MAJOR (0/8, 4/8, 8/8)

The strongest Support & Resistance. 4/8 is the "Magnet" midpoint. Expect heavy consolidation or violent reversals here.

SECONDARY (2/8, 6/8)

Key pivot zones. If price breaks these, it usually runs to the next Major level.

MINOR (1/8, 3/8, 5/8, 7/8)

Intraday scalping pivots. Used for trailing stops or aggressive "Zone-to-Zone" scalps.

High-Probability Setups

★★★★★

The 4/8 Mean Reversion

Wait for price to over-extend to 8/8 or 0/8, then trade the move back to the 4/8 Midpoint Magnet.

★★★★★

Breakout & Retest

Price breaks a Major level (e.g., 8/8), closes above, and returns to "kiss" the level. Enter on the rejection candle.

★★★★☆

The Octave Runner

When momentum is high, trade the "clean air" between 2/8 and 4/8 or 4/8 and 6/8. These are fast moves.

Execution Rules

  • The 5-Min Close: Never enter a trade before the 5-minute candle closes outside or on the zone.
  • Volume Confirmation: Look for relative volume expansion when price approaches Major levels.
  • Stop Loss: Always place stops 1-2 points beyond the zone band, never exactly on the line.
The Lunch Rule: Avoid new entries between 11:30 AM and 1:30 PM EST. Volume usually dies, leading to GANN level "chopping."

Risk Management

The 2% Rule

Risk max 2% of equity per trade. If you hit -4% in a day, stop trading immediately.

Scaling Out

Take 50% profit at the first GANN level. Move stop to Break Even. Let the remaining 50% run to the next Major level.
